Quran Tajweed & Tarteel

Our Tajweed and Quran classes are offered in class with three Levels (Basic, Intermediate, Advance)

Alhuda Community Learning Center Tajweed program, which brings a unique method of learning Quran with proper Tajweed, (recite the Qur'an with proper pronunciation as well as recitation at a moderate speed) designed for youth and adults.

This program is for people who have some background in Arabic, as well as for those who have no background in the Arabic language at all.

Basic

The Alphabet, Combined Letters, Letters Pronounced Separately

  • Short Vowels (Fatha, Dhamma, Kasrah), Doubled Vowels, Miniature Long Vowels, Long Vowels and Soft Letters, Vowel-less Letters (Sukoon), Doubled Letters (Shaddah)
  • Exercises combining letters/words and of the previous lessons
  • Pronunciation and Articulation of each letter (Makharij)
  • Writing Practice
  • Complete IQRA and start reading from Mushaf
  • Complete reading of 1 Juz (minimum)
  • Tarbiyah [Basic Manners of ISLAM and DUA
  • Enroll
Next

Intermediate

Continue to go over the rules of pronunciation and articulation. We will go over the Tafkheem and Tarqeeq of Letters (lightness and heaviness) Students will learn and implement rules of Tajweed Huroof Al- Madiyyah (long letter)

  • Madd-al- Leen, Noon and Meem Mushaddad, Al- Madd (different mudood ), Qalqalah
  • Noon sakinah and Tanween (Idgham, Ikhfa, Idhar), Al-Iqlab
  • Meem Sakinah (Idgham, Ikhfa, Idhar)
  • Hamzah Al- Wasl, Al- Waqf (the stop)
  • Script Writing, Read from Mushaf fluently
  • Complete recitation of 8 Juz (minimum).
  • Students can continue to complete the khatam of the Quran at this level
  • Tarbiyah [Basic Manners of ISLAM and DUA
  • Enroll
Next

Advance

Continue to go over the rules of pronunciation and articulation and also tajweed rules. Along with Complete recitation of Quran

  • Read from Mushaf fluently
  • Learn techniques to memorize
  • Pronunciation and Articulation of each letter (Makharij)
  • Memorize at least 1 Juz (minimum)
  • Tarbiyah [Basic Manners of ISLAM and DUA
  • Enroll

Hifzh Program – Full Time

This is a full time program (5 hrs per day), dedicated to helping students master the memorization and recitation of the Quran.

Student Must satisfy the Advanced Level program to qualify
Student will be trained to write Surahs from Musaff.
Memorization and Revision through Writing with Individual Attention and Daily Revision

Daily classes are structured in to three phase with 2 breaks in between.

  • Phase 1: New Lesson - First time memorization
  • Phase 2: Previous Lessons - Recent memorization
  • Phase 3: Revision of old Lessons
